QR Studio

Premium QR Code Generator — v2.0

QR Studio is a modern, professional-grade QR code generation platform designed for creators, businesses, and developers worldwide. Built with a focus on speed, simplicity, and design excellence, QR Studio empowers you to create beautiful, fully customizable QR codes in seconds — all directly in your browser.

Whether you need a quick URL code, a detailed vCard for networking events, a WiFi auto-connect badge for your office, or a branded marketing link — QR Studio delivers with unmatched ease of use and pixel-perfect output. Our platform supports 20+ QR types across multiple categories, ensuring you always have the right tool for the job.

Every QR code is generated locally in your browser using cutting-edge web technologies. There are no servers processing your data, no uploads, and no accounts required. Your content stays private, and your codes are ready to share instantly. From small businesses to global enterprises, QR Studio is the trusted choice for millions of creators around the world.

Your Privacy Matters

QR Studio is designed with a privacy-first architecture. All QR code generation happens entirely within your browser using client-side JavaScript. No data is ever transmitted to any server during the QR creation process.

Data Collection

  • We do not collect, store, or process any personal information.
  • QR code content (URLs, text, contact info, etc.) is processed locally in your browser and never leaves your device.
  • We do not track, log, or monitor the content of any QR codes you generate or scan.
  • History data is stored only in your browser's local storage and can be cleared at any time from Settings.

Cookies & Analytics

QR Studio uses minimal cookies only for storing your theme preference and language selection. We may use anonymous, aggregated analytics (such as Google Analytics or Google AdSense) to understand general usage patterns and improve the platform. These services may place their own cookies. No personally identifiable information is collected through these services.

Third-Party Services

QR Studio may include third-party scripts for advertising (Google AdSense) and font loading (Google Fonts). These services operate under their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the respective privacy policies of these providers.

Disclaimer

QR Studio is provided "as is" without warranty of any kind. While we strive to ensure reliable QR code generation, we are not responsible for the content encoded in QR codes created by users or the functionality of scanned links. Users are solely responsible for the content they generate and distribute.
